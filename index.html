China

Chinese construction equipment pricing remained steady versus the previous quarter. Despite concerns of a slowdown in the Chinese economy caused by both government reform and global economic malaise, 65% of contacts reported that construction equipment pricing remained steady.


Crane pricing was off slightly in China on average for all manufacturers, with 72% of respondents reporting a slight pull-back in customer charges. This was particularly pronounced in coastal cities, but less-so for the countries interior.


Chinese Front-loader pricing was down slightly according to survey respondents, with weakness being most pronounced in coastal cities. Interior projects were cited as propping up pricing away from the coast.


Backhoe pricing was up slightly ahead of road-building in the interior of the country continuing to gain momentum. The pricing was seen as positive for both domestic and foreign manufacturers across most grades of backhoes.
